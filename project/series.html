The Y-Series:
Thailand's most improbable export.

A genre Thai studios invented for a domestic teenage audience and which the rest of the world adopted with no warning. Today, "Y" (from Japanese yaoi) is a $400M ecosystem of romantic dramas between male leads — broadcast across 50+ countries, with fandoms larger than the country that made them.
