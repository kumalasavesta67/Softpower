The Premise

Thai films and series
are a form of diplomacy.

While Thai food, Muay Thai, and cultural heritage have traveled abroad for decades — what has recently become the most powerful trend is the storytelling from Thai cinema and series.

Ghost stories that are both humorous and horrifying, high schools that become heist thrillers, ancient love legends turned blockbusters, and teen series that change how the world understands Southeast Asia — all of this is "Soft Power" in its purest sense.
