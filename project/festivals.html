Visit · The Calendar

Six festivals
worth flying for.

Bangkok and Chiang Mai now host a year-round film calendar. Six events have established themselves as essential — combining premieres, retrospectives, and industry markets.

Bangkok · International

Bangkok International
Film Festival

Bangkok International Film Festival

Re-launched in 2024 after a fifteen-year hiatus. Eight programme strands and a regional industry market. The country's flagship.

SeptemberBKK · CentralWorld
Bangkok · World Cinema

World Film
Festival of Bangkok

World Film Festival of Bangkok

The country's longest-running curator-led festival. Discovery-focused — first stop in Asia for many European art-house premieres.

NovemberBKK · House Samyan
Chiang Mai · Documentary

CAAT
Documentary Festival

Chiang Mai Documentary Festival

Boutique documentary festival in northern Thailand. Programmed by filmmakers, for filmmakers. SEA non-fiction's regional anchor.

FebruaryChiang Mai · Old Town
Bangkok · Short Form

Short Film &
Video Festival

Short Film Festival

Run by the Thai Film Archive since 1997. Annual showcase of the country's shorts, music videos, and student work. Pipeline for new directors.

AugustSalaya · Film Archive
Bangkok · Genre

Bangkok Asian
Film Festival

Bangkok Asian Film Festival

Pan-Asian genre specialist. Horror, action, romance. The festival where Thai mainstream production talks to Korean, Indonesian, and Vietnamese counterparts.

JulyBKK · Major Cineplex
Phuket · Open-Air

Andaman Film
Days

Andaman Film Festival

Beach-side open-air programme. Three nights, three films, sea breeze, sticky rice. The most photographable cinema event in the country.

MarchPhuket · Karon Beach
